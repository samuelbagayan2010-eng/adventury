Palawan (VISAYAS)

Palawan is a breathtaking archipelagic province in the western Philippines, widely celebrated as the country's "Last Ecological Frontier".

Stretching over 400 kilometers, it features pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, dramatic limestone karst formations, and some of the highest marine biodiversity in the world. An escape to El Nido delivers a stunning, raw nature experience unlike any other destination on earth.

Highlight: Limestone Karsts & Turquoise lagoons
Activities: Island Hopping, Kayaking, Snorkeling
